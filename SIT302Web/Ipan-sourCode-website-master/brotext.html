BROCCOLI



Varieties include:

Select:

Choose compact heads of broccoli with a blue/green colour.

Store:

In an airtight bag in the refrigerator crisper. Use soon after purchase.

Cooking:

Boil, steam or microwave (with 2 tablespoons of water on high) until tender and bright in colour.

Serving suggestion:

Use in stir-fries, salads, vegetable side dishes and frittatas (try the vegie frittata recipe we’ve included with this newsletter).

Why eat broccoli?



Broccoli is packed full of vitamin A, E, C, folate and fibre, as well as being a great immunity booster. Look back over ‘Everyday health benefits of vegetables from Newsletter 1 for other health benefits of vegetables.
